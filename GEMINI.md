# CompTIA Security+ Study Instructions

This workspace is dedicated to Obsidian .md notes for the CompTIA Security+ exam preparation.

## Study Prioritization & Domain Focus

Follow this order of operations for note creation and review, prioritized by exam weight and complexity:

1. **Domain 4: Operations and Incident Response (28%)** - *Highest Priority*
   - Focus: Incident Response (IR) phases, IAM tools, vulnerability management lifecycle.
   - Note: Most exam questions are sourced from this domain.

2. **Domain 2: Architecture and Design (22%)**
   - Focus: Memorize attack types and social engineering variants.
   - Note: Expect heavy scenario-based questions.

3. **Domain 5: Governance, Risk, and Compliance (20%)**
   - Focus: Risk strategies (Accept, Avoid, Transfer, Mitigate) and industry-specific compliance laws.

4. **Domain 3: Implementation (18%)**
   - Focus: Cloud shared responsibility model, RPO vs. RTO, and site types (Hot/Warm/Cold).

5. **Domain 1: Attacks, Threats, and Vulnerabilities (12%)** - *Foundational/Review*
   - Focus: Control types, CIA triad, and AAA.
   - Strategy: Study on day one for foundation, and review on the final day for quick recall.
