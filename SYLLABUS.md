# CompTIA Security+ SYLLABUS & RESOURCES

## Exam Domains

- Domain 1: General Security Concepts (12%)
- Domain 2: Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations (22%)
- Domain 3: Security Architecture (18%)
- Domain 4: Security Operations (28%)
- Domain 5: Program Management & Oversight (20%)

---

## Study Resources by Domain

### Domain 1 — General Security Concepts
*   **Professor Messer:** Excellent video coverage.
*   **[NIST Glossary](https://csrc.nist.gov/glossary):** Free, authoritative definitions for CIA, AAA, and cryptography terms.

### Domain 2 — Threats, Vulnerabilities, Mitigations
*   **[MITRE ATT&CK](https://attack.mitre.org/):** Real-world threat actor tactics/techniques mapped to exam topics.
*   **[CISA.gov Alerts](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/cybersecurity-advisories):** Free real-world threat intelligence and active alerts.

### Domain 3 — Security Architecture
*   **Cloud Provider Docs:** [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/compliance/shared-responsibility-model/), [Azure](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/security/fundamentals/shared-responsibility), and [Google Cloud](https://cloud.google.com/architecture/framework/security/shared-responsibility-model) free tier docs for shared responsibility (IaaS/PaaS/SaaS).
*   **[NIST SP 800-145](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-145/final):** The official definition of Cloud Computing (Free PDF).

### Domain 4 — Security Operations
*   **[TryHackMe](https://tryhackme.com/):** Free tier rooms for IR, SIEM, log analysis, firewalls, and IAM. Hands-on experience for the heaviest domain.
*   **[Cybrary](https://www.cybrary.it/):** Free tier courses for operations and incident response.

### Domain 5 — Program Management & Oversight
*   **[NIST SP 800-30](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-30/rev-1/final):** Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments (Free PDF).
*   **[CompTIA Exam Objectives](https://www.comptia.org/training/resources/exam-objectives):** Download the official PDF; it lists every compliance framework required.
